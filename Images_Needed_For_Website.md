Because responsive design requires different aspect ratios, each category includes an **Aspect Ratio** note:
- **PC/Desktop (Landscape):** Use `--ar 16:9` or `--ar 3:2` at the end of your Midjourney prompt.
- **Android/Mobile (Portrait):** Use `--ar 9:16` or `--ar 4:5` at the end of your Midjourney prompt.

### Tips for Generating:
1. **Consistency:** Add the phrase `"corporate color palette of royal blue, white, and subtle gold"` to the prompts if you want them to perfectly match the new website theme.
2. **Text:** Avoid asking AI image generators to include specific words or logos (like "Aryian's"), as they often spell things wrong. Generate clean images with empty space where text can be overlaid on the website.
3. **Upscaling:** Since these are for a premium website, make sure to use the "Upscale" feature in your generator to ensure the images are crisp on 4K monitors.
